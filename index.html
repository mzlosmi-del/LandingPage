Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

Can it handle the actual complexity of my product line?

Yes. Konfigurator handles dependent characteristics, surface-area pricing, conditional surcharges, per-language labels, and display rules per product. If your sales team can describe it in a spreadsheet, the formula engine can model it.

Will it work on my existing website?

It embeds as a single script tag, a Web Component, or an iFrame — covering WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Wix, and any custom CMS. Your developer won't need to rebuild anything.

What happens to the inquiries — does this replace my sales team?

No. Inquiries land in your dashboard with the full configuration, customer details, and computed price. Your team converts them into formal quotes and follows up. Konfigurator removes the back-and-forth on basic specs so your people spend time on real conversations.

Can it push data into my ERP or CRM?

Yes. HMAC-signed webhooks fire on every inquiry and quotation status change. Native Zapier, HubSpot, and Pipedrive integrations are shipping during early access. Custom integrations are straightforward via the webhook payload.

Where do my 3D models come from?

Upload your own GLB or GLTF files — most CAD packages export them. For early-access customers, we'll help convert one model from your existing assets so you launch with a real preview on day one.
